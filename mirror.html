You notice your reflection in the mirror.

Honestly, even after the chaos of the harvest and foraging for the winter, me looks are just fine.

Your gaze falls upon the scar that mars your left eye. Straight down your brow, tapering off just above your cheek.

Nothing the village asks of me is going to make me look worse than that, I suppose.

Memories of the past had long lain dormant within that scar, but now they began to flood your mind. The bridge at Münden. Hrungnir and his wretched taskmaster. The Three Billy Goats.

It serves as a stern reminder of your past as "The troll who collected the toll".

It was a “toll” that you didn't simply collect, but paid yourself.

...An eye for me freedom.
