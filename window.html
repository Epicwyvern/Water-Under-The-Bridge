You peer out of the window to see Hessen enjoying a late autumn afternoon. The sky is slowly darkening, and zwielicht fast approaches.

Far off from the west you hear the yelling of a concerned mother, with bits of childish laughter in between.

Must be Heinrich playing in the pond again, that little...

Fallen leaves eddy in the chill breeze from the north. Another week or so and you might see the first snows of winter.

You weren't particularly fond of the winter. "Seasons" that "change" are a novelty for you; the world beyond the bridge wasn't always snowing. You want to enjoy the reds, yellows, and browns a little bit more before the monotonous white.

Oh well, spring should come soon enough.
