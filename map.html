...but where was Steckelberg again?

You still aren't used to the south. Being stuck at the bridge up north for such an age made it hard to grasp the lay of the land.

You squint at the map of Germania until you make out Hessen, with Steckelberg lying just below.

Ah, there it is!

You trace your way from Hessen to Steckelberg.

First to Fuld down the river Fulda, and then to Ostheim. 10 leagues southwest from there...

As you continue thinking about the path to Steckelberg, you begin tracing backwards along the river Fulda, past Fuld and even Hessen. At one moment in time, you used to guard the bridge crossing over it at Munden, stopping many a merchant on their ventures.

In another, the Fulda carried you from your fall all the way to Hessen. In some twist of fate, you seem to be in the merchant's shoes now.

The harvest is mostly dealt with, so I'll talk with Faldor and set out first thing in the morning.
